ABOUT THE TRUST

Pune Panjarpol Trust in Bhosari (District Pune) is an organization working in cow protection and conservation since 1855. The organization has started the work of Jativant Desi Cattle Conservation and Breeding. In Panjarpol, organic fertilizers, vermicomposting, dasaparni extract, biocides are produced from dung, cow urine. Local farmers buy these products. The organization has taken up the work of Jativant cow conservation and propagation in collaboration with various NGOs.

Pune Panjarpol Trust was established in the year 1855. The office of the organization was started at Narayan Peth in Pune city. The nature of the organization's work is protection and conservation of cows. The primary objective of the organization is to take care of cows, buffaloes and bullocks who have been injured in road accidents. While giving information about the organization's activities, Purushottam Ladha said that in 1944, the organization arranged cowsheds in Bhosari on an area of ​​ten acres for the rearing of various breeds of cows.
